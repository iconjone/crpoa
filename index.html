Architectural Control
Committee
Purpose:

To represent the Carrington Ranch Home Owner’s Association in viewing construction documents for conformity to the requirements of the Covenants, as filed with the Williamson County Court House, and for maintaining the architectural intent originally established by Walter Carrington.

2016-2017 Committee Members:
Steve Gonzales, Sean Cockburn, Jeremy Morris

EMAIL
New Home
Construction
Construction documents must be submitted to the ACC for review, comment and approval as outlined in the Covenants.

The document package must comply with all the data requirements of the Checklist or Construction Plans document. Packages will NOT be accepted if the package is incomplete.

Required documents:

APPLICATION FOR REVIEW OF CONSTRUCTION CHECKLIST FOR CONSTRUCTION PLANS
Variance Letter (if Homeowner needs a variance from established Covenants and Restrictions)
Please NOTE: All documents submitted to the ACC shall remain the property of said committee.
